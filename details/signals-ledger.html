Product System

Signals Ledger

An internal product interface recast as an editorial ledger for monitoring signals, annotations, and decision context without the visual weight of an operational dashboard.

Role
Interface design, front-end prototype, content modeling, and review facilitation.
Scope
Ledger views, annotation rules, signal grouping, lightweight stats, and discussion-ready presentation.
Stack
Semantic HTML, shared CSS tokens, minimal JavaScript interactions, and structured editorial labels.
Outcome
Stakeholders could read change over time, attach commentary, and review decisions with less meeting overhead.

Challenge

Reduce dashboard fatigue

The original tool carried too much chrome and too little explanatory structure for strategic review work.

Structure

Turn updates into readable records

Signals were grouped into linked records with source, annotation, confidence, and traceable editorial context.

Interaction

Keep the interface calm under load

Counts, state markers, and filters stayed present but subdued so the text remained the primary surface.

Result

Sharper review and slower mistakes

The ledger made assumptions visible, which improved critique quality and helped teams avoid hasty interpretation.
